Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Indian cricketer

Serving for the team as an batting Wicket Keeper.

Acheivements

  • Led India to the inaugural ICC World Twenty20 title in 2007.
  • Captained the Indian team to the No. 1 spot in Test rankings for the first time in December 2009.
  • Guided India to the ICC Cricket World Cup victory in 2011, ending a 28-year-long drought for the nation.
  • Captained India to victory in the ICC Champions Trophy in 2013, making India the first team to win all major ICC trophies.
  • Led CSK to victory, securing 5 IPL trophies, a testament to excellence.
